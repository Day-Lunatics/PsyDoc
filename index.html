PsyDoc

Discord BOT that survey you to inform you if you need help from professional theraphist

About

PsyDoc is a Discord bot that give an private mean to survey yourself. If you don't know, if you have a problem with: depression, alcoholism etc.

This bot respect your privacy because of this all messages after inicializng contact on the channel are sent as private messages to you. We don't collect your data or save them anywhere so you don't have to worry about your data being used agains you in some maliciouse way.

This project is free to all and made by people that are not professional therapist soo what we offer is just a survey and encourage to contact professional and seek help from them.

How it works

This bot works on the predefined questions that are choosen randomly and based on your answers you get the information if you should contact professional help.

Type $info to get information about PsyDoc

Type $contact_me to get private message with instructions what to do next
